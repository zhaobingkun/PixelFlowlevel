Turn Hard Pixel Flow Levels Into Repeatable Patterns

Challenging levels feel easier when you identify repeatable structures and mirror moves.

2025-11-28 • 6 min read • Strategy

Mirror the grid

If a level looks symmetrical, solve one side and mirror the moves.

Name the blocks

Assign short labels to tricky blocks so you can track them in your head.

Lock the anchors

Secure fixed paths first, then weave flexible ones around them.

Quick takeaways

  • Symmetry often means the same move twice.
  • Use the guide to confirm anchor paths.
  • Stop after a clean run and save the pattern.
